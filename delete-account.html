Delete Your Account

How to request account deletion

If you would like to delete your Dizzy account and all associated data, you can do so using one of the following methods:

  1. In-app: Open Dizzy, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account, and confirm the deletion.
  2. By email: Send an email to info@dizzy-app.com with the subject line "Delete My Account". Include the email address associated with your Dizzy account.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

Data type Action Timeframe
Account information (email, name) Deleted Immediately
Saved styles and preferences Deleted Immediately
Subscription data Deleted Immediately
Anonymous analytics data Auto-deleted Within 60 days

Processing time

Email requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.

Important notes

Deleting your account will cancel any active subscriptions. You will lose access to all saved styles and preferences. This action cannot be undone.

If you have questions, contact us at info@dizzy-app.com.
